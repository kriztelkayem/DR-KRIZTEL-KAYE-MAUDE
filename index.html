About Dr. Kriztel Maude

Dr. Kriztel Kaye A. Maude, MD

Radiologist Specialist

Dr. Maude grew up in Malungon, Sarangani Province, where her passion for helping others began at an early age. Inspired by her community's need for accessible healthcare, she pursued her dream of becoming a doctor.

She completed her medical degree at the Doctors Medical School Foundation, where she built a strong foundation in clinical knowledge and patient care. She further advanced her expertise by earning her doctorate at MSU General Santos, one of the leading institutions in the region.

Today, Dr. Maude is known for her compassionate approach, professionalism, and dedication to improving the well-being of her patients. She believes that healthcare is not just about treatment, but also about education, prevention, and trust.
